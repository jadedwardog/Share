# **User Specification**

* Users create accounts by generating a recovery keypair.  
* Recovery key is the sole authority for posting and account control.  
* Users are responsible for safeguarding recovery keys; loss of recovery key results in permanent account inertness.  
* Users may post content encrypted per-post with AEAD keys.  
* Users select content licenses independently (e.g., CC-BY, CC0, proprietary) as defined in CONTENT\_LICENSE.md.  
* Users may follow other identities and receive summaries of new content.  
* Users may participate in community votes for supplemental guidelines and council dissolution, as defined in GOVERNANCE\_CHARTER.md.  
* Users may appeal council decisions through new proposals and votes.  
* Users are responsible for respecting bandwidth limitations and privacy rules enforced by clients.  
* Users must comply with global guidelines defined in the manifesto and supplemental guidelines ratified by community vote.
